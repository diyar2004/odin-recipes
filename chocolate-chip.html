Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious cookies only made in 4 steps!

Ingredients

  • Crisco baking sticks
  • brown sugar
  • milk
  • vanilla extract
  • eggs
  • flour
  • salt
  • baking soda
  • chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  2. COMBINE shortening, brown sugar,milk and vanilla in a large bowl. Beat at medium speed of electric mixer until well blended. Beat in egg. combine flour, salt and baking soda. Mix into shortening mixture until just blended. Stir in chocolate chips.
  3. Drop by rounded measuring tablespoons 3 inches apart onto a Ungreased baking sheet.
  4. BAKE 8 to 10 minutes for chewy cookies, or 11 to 13 minutes for crisp cookies. Cool 2 minutes on cooling racks.
